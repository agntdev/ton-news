# Ton News content calendar

This calendar gives editors a repeatable publishing cadence for TON ecosystem
coverage. It is designed for a small team that wants consistent output without
turning every article into breaking-news work.

## Publishing cadence

| Day | Content type | Owner | Goal |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Monday | Weekly TON ecosystem brief | Editor | Summarize protocol, tooling, wallet, and app updates. |
| Wednesday | Explainer or guide | Writer | Answer one search-driven question in depth. |
| Friday | Builder or project spotlight | Writer | Profile a useful app, SDK, validator tool, or community project. |
| Monthly | Data review | Editor | Review validator, network, DeFi, or developer activity trends. |

## Topic pillars

- Protocol and network upgrades
- Validator operations and staking
- Wallets, payments, and onboarding
- DeFi, gaming, social, and consumer apps on TON
- Developer tooling, SDKs, APIs, and smart contract patterns
- Security, reliability, and ecosystem risk notes
- Community grants, hackathons, and builder resources

## Calendar maintenance

- Review the next four weeks every Monday.
- Keep at least two evergreen explainers ready as backup posts.
- Avoid publishing unsourced rumors; use a short brief only when primary
  sources are available.
- Update completed rows with final URLs after publication.
